About Us

Alison Exim is one of the leading enterprises operating in the city of ​Brooklyn. The company serves as a trustworthy supplier of various products. The list of products we provide includes allopathic syrup, energy drink, alcohol, face masks, dry fruits, edible oils, and fresh eggs. Apart from these, we also supply sanitizer, charcoal, onion, incense sticks, halal froze chicken, and more products. The wide range of different products that we provide is manufactured by leading producers in the market. Hence, you can stay assured of the quality of different products offered by us.

The Storage

We have a spacious storehouse for storing allopathic syrup, energy drink, alcohol, face masks, dry fruits, edible oils, fresh eggs, sanitizer, charcoal, onion, incense sticks, halal froze chicken, and more products. All eatables are stored in cold storage section so that the taste doesn’t degrade.

Quality Check

We have an R&D team that performs the quality check of all products. According to the set of international parameters, we determine the quality. All clients receive only those products that have successfully matched or surpassed the parameters.
